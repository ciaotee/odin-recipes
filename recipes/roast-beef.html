Hawaiian Chicken


Description

Delicious chicken. Serve over rice. Smells amazing.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds chicken breast
  • 1 (18 ounce) bottle honey barbecue sauce
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) can sliced pineapple
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce

Steps

  1. Put chicken breasts into the crock of a slow cooker; top with barbeque sauce, pineapple slices, and soy sauce.
  2. Cook on High for 4 to 5 hours.
